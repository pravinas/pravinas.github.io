Who Am I?

Hiya! I'm Pravi, PR4v1, pravinas, RobotNerd, or whatever else you want to call me. I am a first year PhD student, studying robotics under Dr. Sabrina Neuman at Boston University. I hope that I make your day a little bit brighter, and a whole lot weirder.
